About Me

Sherry has 17 years of experience in the Telecommunications industry, primarily focused in the areas of project management and management. She holds a B.A. in Psychology from the University of Michigan and an M.B.A in Marketing from George Washington University.

Sherry loves tennis and skiing and is passionate about the benefits of maintaining a healthy lifestyle. She has five years of experience as a small business owner, providing nutritious baking mixes to the healthy food community.
